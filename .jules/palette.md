## 2025-03-11 - [Shopping Cart Accessibility & I18n]
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like quantity +/- and trash icons) require both `aria-label` for screen readers and `Tooltip` for visual context to be fully accessible and user-friendly. Explicit `htmlFor`/`id` pairing on forms ensures assistive technology can correctly identify input purposes. Additionally, transcription errors in specialized character sets (like Greek 'γγ' being typed as 'gg') can easily occur in localized files and should be audited.
**Action:** Always wrap icon-only buttons in `Tooltip` and provide a localized `aria-label`. Use a consistent pattern for form label pairing and verify non-Latin script translations for transcription artifacts.
